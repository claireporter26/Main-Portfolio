Hi, I'm Claire.   I'm a Senior Software Engineer and originally self taught web developer based in West Sussex.

Over the past several years I have been able to build on these skills in a job role which covered many areas of web development and working with multiple CMS platforms, working with a blend of stakeholders and teams within the company, and working on a variety of projects from building and improving websites to implementing Adobe Analytics for improved tracking of our customer behaviour. I am starting to incorporate AI into my work processes, using software such as Claude and Microsoft 365 chats and agents to help speed up bug fixes and provide ideas for code improvements, along with assisting with communication across various colleagues, both technical and non-technical.

I also have another love in my life; my little boy was born in 2019. It's been a balancing act to spend enough time with my family and still find the time I need to continue my programming journey, but it's worth the journey.

As in all areas of life, there is always more the learn and I look forward to continue working with new software, improving my profiency in the the most widely used coding languages while also keeping myself aware of the latest trends, and the exciting future of AI and how it can improve how we work as Software Engineers.
