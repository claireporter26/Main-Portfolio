Hi I'm Claire.     I am a self taught web developer based in West Sussex. I started learning to code a few years ago after my an online video caught my interest. I started with HTML and then soon started to delve into the world of CSS. I knew that I had found something I was passionate about because the desire to study was strong enough to get me up early in the morning!

The next language I started to learn was Javascript, but while I was learning this my life dramatically changed as I had my little baby boy. In the last few months I have been able to find the time to continue my studies more regularly which I am really excited about.

My current focus is to improve my JavaScript proficiency to allow me to really excel at Front End Development. My long term aim is to learn enough to become a Full Stack developer and I'm looking forward to discover which direction my passion takes me from there.
